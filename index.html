Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues
  • Chameleons take a while to hatch and don’t live very long

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Pandas are the most expensive animals to be kept in zoos or captivity.
  2. Pandas are actually bears.
  3. Unlike most other bears, pandas don’t hibernate.
